중국 신신화 2021 The New Chinese Myth of 2021

천거
Ge Chen
평소에 여러분이 보시는 중국 신화 주제의일러스트레이션은 대부분 매우 전통적이고 사실적으로 표현되어 있으며 대부분의 화면은 중국의 전통 대표 색채와 전통 회화 기법을 응용하여 표현되었다. 이로써 이번 작업에서는 기존의 중국식 화풍을 바꾸어 서양의 일러스트레이션의 편평풍 화풍을 이용하여 동양 신화의 검고 단순한 선과 순수한 색상으로 주제를 표현할려고 한다. 이번 일러스트레이션 설정은 '신화적 인물이 고대에서 현대로 온다면 그들은 현대에서 무엇을 할 것인가'라는 내용이다. 창작 아이디어로서 언어가 아닌 직관적인 이미지를 통해 시각적 전달을 하는 것도 외국인들이 우리 중국의 우수한 전통문화를 더 잘 이해할 수 있도록 하는 것입니다
I believe that most of the illustrations that you see on the theme of Chinese mythology are more traditional and realistic. Inspired by this, this illustration changed the traditional Chinese style of painting, using the flat style of the West to narrate the Eastern mythology, black thick simple lines and the use of pure colors to express the theme. This time the illustration sets "If mythical characters came from ancient times to modern times, what would they do in modern times?" As a creative idea, visual communication through nonverbal and intuitive images is also a way for foreigners to better understand our excellent Chinese traditions. culture

FAMILY

이부분은 가족사진 입니다. 저의 형상 이미지를 가운데로 주위로 둘러싼 현대 신화 속 인물들의 모습입니다. 

블랙과 오렌지가 화면의 메인 컬러인 이 두 컬러는 강한 대비감을 주고 신비감을 줍니다. 제목 밑에는색의 남색으로 차갑고 따뜻한 색채 대비로,단일하지 않고 사람에게 일종의 현대적인 느낌을 가져다 줍니다

This is a family photo with my own image in the middle. Surrounded by the modern mythical figures of my work. Black and orange are the main colors of the picture. The combination of these two colors will produce a strong sense of contrast and a sense of mystery. Then the contrast between the cold and warm blue of the title background color is not uniform, but also brings a modern feel.

CHANG E RUSHES TO THE MOON    嫦娥奔月

“CHANG E RUSHES TO THE MOON” reminds me of China’s Chang’e rocket. Chinese moon exploration rockets are all named “Chang’e.” The ancient Chang’e was flying into the sky to watch the moon. In modern times, Chang’e is directly watching the moon as an astronaut
The pink streamer is likened to bunny ears

IN PURSUIT OF THE SUN    誇父逐日

In ancient times, Kuafu chased the sun with his feet, but in modern times, with the emergence of transportation, I chose bicycles. Because bicycles are a kind of simplicity for people, they can only drive on their own strength. The longer the duration of stamina and endurance, the longer the driving time

COWHERD AND WEAVER GIRL     牛郎織女

The Cowherd and the Weaver Girl can only be seen on July 7th of the lunar calendar every year, just like long-distance couples, it is difficult to see one side.
In front of me, there are individuals with scattered jigsaw puzzles, and the day I meet is like a complete jigsaw puzzle.

HOU YI SHOUT THE SUNS     後羿射日

Inspired by the current state of the new coronavirus. In ancient times, the descendants appeared 10 suns at the same time, which brought disasters to mankind. The descendants shot 9 suns with bows and arrows to save mankind. If the descendants reach the modern age, he compares the bows in the hands of future generations to vaccines to eliminate the virus.

NEZHA CONQUERS THE DRAGON KING      哪咤鬧海

Came to modern Nezha and became a professional surfer. With his knowledge of the sea, he mastered the surfboard skills proficiently.

YU GONG MOVES AWAY THE MOUNTAINS     愚公移山

Yugong’s persistence is worth learning. Yugong moved stones. In modern times, we can compare stones to books. The more we read, the piles of knowledge will definitely help our journey in the future.

NUWA PATCHES UP THE SKY        女娲补天

“NUWA PATCHES UP THE SKY” reminds me of the scene of water leakage on the roof during the flood disaster. Nuwa held the universal tape in her hand to stop the water leakage.

PANGU SEPARATES THE SKY FROM THE EARTH  盘古开天

“PANGU SEPARATES THE SKY FROM THE EARTH” is a newly created environment for mankind. Pangu came to modern times and became a construction worker. Construction workers built high-rise buildings and created a new environment for everyone.

RISE TO THE BAIT    愿者上钩

Jiang Taigong, who “RISE TO THE BAIT”, came to modern times to be an environmental guard. It is everyone’s responsibility to salvage marine debris and protect the environment.
